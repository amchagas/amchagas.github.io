Open Visual Stimulator

andre, maxime, tom, philipp
18 Feb, 2020
Code

Built as a collaboration of the Baden lab and the Euler Lab, this spatial stimulator is able to use most of the commercially available LEDs, making it quite flexible in terms of wavelength spectra. It can take up to 6 different LEDs and be synchronised with most microscope systems.
